Do's & Don'ts

When in Rome, do as the Romans do!

Do's

  • Dress fashionably
  • Eat gelato. Lots of it
  • Go a few blocks away from popular attractions for an authentic meal
  • Drink coffee at the bar - it's cheaper!
  • Try to learn some of the language
  • Explore the path less taken

Dont's

  • Drink cappuccinos after 11am
  • Tip your server; practice is to round up to the nearest 5 or 10
  • Expose your shoulders when in church
  • Eat at restaurants near tourist attractions
  • Don't let your wallet/important articles out of your hands or sight!
  • Don't expect every restaurant/store to be open during siesta time (1-4pm)
