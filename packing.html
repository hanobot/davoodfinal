What to bring

When in Rome, do as the Romans do!

Essentials

  • Passport (make sure it isn't expired!)
  • Cash
  • Credit card with no foreign transaction fee
  • Charger adapter

Clothes & Accessories

  • Fashionable but light and breathable shirts
  • Comfortable walking shoes. Cobblestone is everywhere
  • A hat to block out the sun! Preferably not a baseball cap
  • Small crossbody bag that zips, no buttons those are easier to pickpocket!
  • Colorful jewelry always brings a plain outfit a step up
  • For ladies, dresses that aren't too short. For gentlemen, a pair of slacks or linen pants would do

Others

  • Reusable water bottle
  • Neck pillow for the long flight over
  • Sunscreen
  • Camera along with film, memory cards, chargers, etc.
